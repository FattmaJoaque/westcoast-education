About Westcoast Education

Our Legacy

Founded in 1985, Westcoast Education has been at the forefront of IT education for nearly 40 years. What started as a small classroom initiative just north of Gothenburg has grown into one of Sweden's most respected technical training providers.

Our Mission

We specialize in system development with a focus on web and mobile solutions. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical, industry-ready skills. We don't just teach code – we shape developers who can think, create, and innovate.

Our Evolution

While traditional classroom training remains in our DNA, we're embracing the future with our new on-demand platform. Whether you prefer face-to-face interaction, distance learning, or self-paced video courses, Westcoast Education has the perfect learning path for you.
